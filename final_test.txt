**Brands:** Brufen, Ibubid, Advil, Motrin

**Uses:**
  • Pain relief
  • Fever reduction
  • Anti-inflammatory (joint pain, muscle pain, period pain)

**Dosage (General):**
  Adults: 200-400 mg every 6-8 hours with food (max 1200 mg/day OTC).

**Side Effects (Common):**
  ⚠️ Stomach upset/heartburn
  ⚠️ Can cause gastric ulcers with long use
  ⚠️ Dizziness
  ⚠️ Fluid retention

**Contraindications:**
  ✗ Stomach ulcers
  ✗ Kidney disease
  ✗ Third trimester of pregnancy
  ✗ Aspirin allergy
  ✗ Heart disease (caution)
